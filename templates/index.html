About TED and TEDx

TED is a global foundation devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. It started out in 1984 as a conference bringing together people from three worlds: Technology, Entertainment, and Design. Since then, its scope has become ever broader. With its two annual conferences, video website TED.com, initiatives, and community projects, TED is now a platform and a global movement focused on meaningful change through the power of ideas.
TEDx events are grassroots, community-driven gatherings that facilitate deep conversations and connections, fueled by the belief that ideas have the potential to bring about positive change. Organized independently, these events aim to inspire innovation, thought leadership, and visionary insights, while remaining free from political motivations, financial rewards, or personal gains
About TedxIITBombay

TEDxIITBombay

TEDx IIT Bombay is an independently organized TED event that will take place at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay). TEDx events are local, self-organized conferences licensed by TED, where speakers from various fields share their ideas and experiences in the form of short, powerful talks.
The TEDx IIT Bombay features a lineup of speakers who present their ideas and experiences in diverse areas such as technology, science, entrepreneurship, arts, social issues, and more. These talks are designed to inspire and stimulate conversations that can lead to positive change.

TEDxIITBOMBAY THEME

The theme "Conquest for Utopia" revolves around the idea of striving for a better future, aiming to create an ideal society or world. It represents our innate desire to conquer obstacles, both external and internal,encourages people to imagine and strive for a better future.The aim is to inspire and empower individuals to actively participate in the conquest for a more utopian world by sharing ideas, sparking dialogue, and fostering collaboration.
